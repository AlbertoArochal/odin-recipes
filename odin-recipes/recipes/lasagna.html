Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 500grs of meat
  • 2 red peppers
  • 2 carrots
  • 150grs of bacon
  • 3 medium size onions
  • 250grs tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of white wine
  • 2 spoons of olive oil
  • 1 tea spoon of oregano
  • salt and pepper
  • 12 pasta sheets
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil salted water
  2. Turn of the fire
  3. Put the pasta sheets inside the watter for 20 minutes
  4. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned
  5. Stir in tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  7. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking
  8. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture
  9. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella
  10. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking
  11. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes
  12. EAT
